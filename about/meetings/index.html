About

What?

Rochester 2600 is a local chapter of the 2600 Hacker Quarterly. It’s a loose-knit group that hangs out, does presentations related to security and technology, and discusses a variety of topics throughout the night.

When?

Meetings occur the first Friday of every month at 7pm

Where?

Interlock Rochester - Rochester’s first and only hackerspace.

1115 E. Main St.
Door #7
Suite 200
Rochester, NY 14609

If the door is not open, you can ring the bell for Interlock Rochester and we will buzz you up.

Who?

The group is made up of local hackers that are interested in network security, physical security, application security, mobile, or any other type of security.

How?

Just show up. Laptops are optional and you don’t need to present if you don’t want.

Why?

We really don’t know.
